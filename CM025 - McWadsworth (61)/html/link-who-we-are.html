Owner

Colin Wadsworth, a chemical engineer turned innovative entrepreneur, discovered his passion for coding during his time as the first employee at a fast-paced startup. Faced with a wide range of responsibilities, he quickly realized the need for efficiency without sacrificing quality. By introducing VBA to automate complex processes in Excel, Colin transformed time-consuming tasks into streamlined workflows, enabling him to juggle multiple roles effectively. This pivotal experience not only elevated his productivity but also opened his eyes to the power of programming as a tool for problem-solving and innovation.

Motivated to deepen his expertise, Colin embarked on a journey to master various programming languages and tools. He began with Python, known for its versatility, and quickly expanded his skill set to include HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and LaTeX. Each new skill brought fresh insights and opportunities, reinforcing his belief that coding is an essential skill in today’s world. Whether developing websites, creating dynamic tools, or preparing professional-grade documents with LaTeX, Colin found coding to be a bridge between ideas and execution.

Fueled by the belief that coding should be accessible to everyone, Colin founded McCode-Wadsworth to break down the barriers surrounding programming. His vision is to demystify the complexities of code and empower individuals from all backgrounds to use it effectively in their everyday lives. Through McCode-Wadsworth, Colin strives to transform technical challenges into intuitive solutions, equipping businesses, students, and professionals with tools and resources that drive innovation and success. For Colin, coding is not just a skill but a means to unlock creativity, solve problems, and shape the future.

Company

McCode-Wadsworth is the gold standard in coding solutions. Just as McMaster-Carr revolutionized the engineering world with its unparalleled quality and reliability, we have redefined what it means to deliver excellence in coding. Our mission is simple: to empower developers, businesses, and students by transforming the complex equations and concepts found in textbooks—ranging from basic algebra to advanced multi-order differential equations—into functional, ready-to-use code. With McCode-Wadsworth, technical concepts are no longer just theoretical; they become tools for innovation and problem-solving.

Our flagship product is a robust library of pre-written code solutions that breathe life into technical material. In addition, we provide an extensive collection of pre-built code modules, hosted on GitHub and curated for a wide variety of use cases. These modules are meticulously researched, expertly crafted, and designed for immediate integration. Simply copy, paste, and execute the code in your Python Integrated Development and Learning Environment (IDLE) or preferred IDE, and it works seamlessly. Whether you’re tackling common challenges or addressing niche requirements, our modules are built to deliver reliability and results.

At McCode-Wadsworth, we offer more than products; we deliver solutions. Our services include custom website development and updates, code integration to streamline workflows, and consultation to optimize operations for businesses of any size. We also provide training designed to ensure our users not only understand how to use code effectively but also grasp the deeper principles that drive successful implementation. We are dedicated to reducing complexity, saving time, and empowering innovation through every interaction.

Excellence is not our aspiration—it’s our standard. McCode-Wadsworth is synonymous with quality, precision, and impact. By offering high-quality tools, real-world solutions, and unmatched expertise, we enable our users to push boundaries, solve complex problems, and lead their industries. When you choose McCode-Wadsworth, you’re not just investing in a tool—you’re joining a community committed to shaping the future of coding. Together, we turn possibilities into realities.
